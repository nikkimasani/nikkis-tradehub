📖 Beginner Foundations

🧠 What Is Day Trading?

Day trading means buying and selling financial instruments within the same trading day. The goal is to profit from short-term price movements.

  • Pattern Day Trader Rule: 4+ trades/5 days needs $25k PDT Rule
  • Most beginners start with paper trading Start Here
  • Risk management is more important than strategy Critical

📊 Key Chart Patterns

Bull Flag
Strong move up, brief consolidation, continuation
Bear Flag
Strong drop, brief rally, continuation down
Cup & Handle
U-shaped base + small pullback = bullish breakout
Head & Shoulders
3-peak pattern — signals trend reversal
Doji
Open ≈ Close — indecision, possible reversal
Hammer
Long lower wick — buyers pushed back sellers

📏 Risk Management Rules

  • Never risk more than 1–2% per trade Rule #1
  • Always set a stop-loss before entering Required
  • Risk/reward ratio minimum 1:2 Target
  • Don't average down on losing trades Avoid
  • Max 3 losing trades = stop for the day Discipline
  • Size position to the stop, not your gut Pro tip

📐 Key Indicators

VWAP

EMA 9/20

RSI
Momentum oscillator. Over 70 overbought, under 30 oversold
Volume
Confirms breakouts. High volume = real move
ATR
Average True Range — helps set stop levels
MACD
Trend + momentum crossover signal

🔤 Trading Glossary

Long
Buying, betting price goes up
Short
Selling borrowed shares, betting price drops
Float
Shares available to trade. Low float = volatile
Spread
Difference between bid and ask price
Support
Price level where buying tends to emerge
Resistance
Price level where selling tends to emerge
Breakout
Price moves through key level with volume
Gap Up/Down
Price opens above/below previous close

🎯 Opening Range Breakout Strategy

  • Wait for first 15–30 min candle to close
  • Mark the high and low of that range
  • Enter on breakout above (long) or below (short)
  • Stop below the range low (for longs)
  • Target 2x the range height minimum
  • Confirm with above-average volume

📘 How to Use TradeLab

A quick tour of every tool in the app — including the newest additions. Everything saves automatically to this browser (and syncs through Supabase when you're signed in).

🔎 Strategy Scanner New

Found on the Today tab. Save a rule-based setup for any watchlist symbol (entry trigger, invalidation, target), then hit Scan.

  • Each symbol is flagged Triggered, Near trigger, Extended, or Invalidated
  • Add symbols to your watchlist first — the scanner reads from it
  • Live quote checks use your Finnhub key (Settings)

💼 Portfolio Simulator New

Also on the Today tab. A local practice portfolio: starting cash, buy/sell orders, open positions, realized and unrealized P&L.

  • Place simulated orders to test ideas without risking money
  • Quotes refresh from Finnhub to mark positions to market
  • Reset any time — it's all local, nothing real is traded

🤖 Bot Scaffolder New

The Bot tab generates starter code for trading automation — pick a strategy, broker/API style, and options, then copy the generated scaffold.

  • Output is a starting point, not a finished bot — review before running
  • Paper-trade any bot before ever going live Critical

🧠 Quiz & Flashcards

Right here in Study. Quick Quiz = 10 random questions; Timed Exam = 25 with a 30s clock; Start Category Quiz runs every question in the selected topic (all 100+ if "All Topics").

  • Weak Spots re-drills the categories you score lowest on
  • Flashcards: tap a card to flip, mark it known to track progress
  • With a Claude API key set, generate fresh AI questions on any topic

📁 Trading Library

The Library tab connects your study materials two ways:

  • Microsoft sign-in browses your OneDrive directly (requires the app's redirect URI to be registered — see the note on the Library page if sign-in errors)
  • Connect Local Folder browses a folder on this computer — works offline, Chrome/Edge only

📊 Today Dashboard

Your pre-market home base: watchlist with notes, earnings calendar, win/loss streak, daily max-loss limit, trading rules checklist, and the pre-market routine checklist.

  • Set a daily max loss — the card turns red when you hit it
  • Keep the watchlist current; the scanner and simulator build on it

📓 P&L Journal & Paper Trade

P&L Journal logs real trades and charts your equity curve and stats. Paper Trade is a structured 90-day challenge tracker.

  • Log every trade — the streak and analytics feed off the journal
  • Journal data also powers the Weak Spots quiz insights

🔑 API Keys (Settings)

Three optional keys unlock live features — each stored only in your browser:

  • Finnhub — live news, earnings, scanner/simulator quotes
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI news briefs and AI quiz questions
  • YouTube — Classroom playlist loading
